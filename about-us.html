ABOUT US

The vision of ARI SHIPYARD is to lead pioneering initiatives for its country and industry, continuing its projects with innovative structures and innovations. The mission of Arı Shipyard is to build ships of excellent quality, high added value, and sophistication. Established on a 20,000-square-meter area with all facilities, Arı Shipyard has the capacity to build fully equipped, specialized ships. With an annual steel processing capacity of 20,000 tons, Arı Shipyard can build ships up to 30,000 DWT to world standards with its experienced workers and staff.
